Dr. Hamachi gave a special lecture on Metal-Organic Frameworks (MOFs) for the ACS Club to explain the science behind the <a href = "https://www.nobelprize.org/prizes/chemistry/2025/summary/">2025 Chemistry Nobel Prize</a>. Dr. Hamachi's current work on Covalent Organic Frameworks (COFs) is related to the MOF research that won the Nobel Prize.

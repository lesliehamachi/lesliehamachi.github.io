Dr. Hamachi co-organized a PMSE symposium titled "Celebrating Polymer Research At Primarily Undergraduate Institutions (PUIs)" at the Atlanta American Chemical Society Meeting. Dr. Hamachi also presented an oral presentation on the group's work on COF Colloid Surface Chemistry.

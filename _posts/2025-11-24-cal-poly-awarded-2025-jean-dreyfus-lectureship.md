Dr. Leslie Hamachi and Cal Poly's Chemistry and Biochemistry department were Awarded a 2025 <a href="https://www.dreyfus.org/jean-dreyfus-lectureship/">Jean Dreyfus Lectureship for Undergraduate Institutions</a>. The Jean Dreyfus Lectureship for Undergraduate Institutions awards provide a $25,000 grant to 1) facilitate a visit and lectures by a notable researcher in the chemical sciences to a primarily undergraduate institution, and 2) fund summer research fellowships for two undergraduate students. Thank you to the The Camille and Henry Dreyfus Foundation for supporting undergraduate research at Cal Poly!

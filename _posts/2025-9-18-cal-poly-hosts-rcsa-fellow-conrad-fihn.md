Dr. Hamachi and the Cal Poly Chemistry and Biochemistry Department hosted RCSA Fellow Conrad Fihn on a Mock Interview. The <a href="https://rescorp.org/rcsa-fellows/">RCSA Fellow Program</a> supports the academic career advancement of a cohort of outstanding postdoctoral scholars over a critical period in their careers: as they are preparing to look for faculty positions; as they are actively interviewing; and their first year as newly independent tenure-track faculty investigators at U.S.

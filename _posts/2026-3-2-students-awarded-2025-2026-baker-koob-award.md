Congratulations to Olive Rosplock, Elian Zurita, and Erin Wang for winning a <a href="https://provost.calpoly.edu/BakerKoob">Baker-Koob Award</a> to fund their student-initiated research project on "Detection and Characterization of Polyvinyl Alcohol in Synthetic Wastewater." Thank you to the Baker-Koob Endowment for your support of student research.

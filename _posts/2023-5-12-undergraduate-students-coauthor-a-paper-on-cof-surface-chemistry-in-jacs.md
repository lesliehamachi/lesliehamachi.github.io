Undergraduate students from the Hamachi Group coauthor a paper with the <a href="https://cohenlab.ucsd.edu/">Cohen Research Group</a> from UCSD on <a href="https://pubs.acs.org/doi/full/10.1021/jacs.5c22900">Probing the External Surface Chemistry of Imine-Based Covalent Organic Frameworks Using Reactive Organic Dyes</a> in the Journal of the American Chemical Society. Congratulations to Cal Poly students Makisig Velasquez and Ava Durbin for publishing your work.
